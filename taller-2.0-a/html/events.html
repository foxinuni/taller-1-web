About GHMU 2025

Gaming Hub's Meet Up 2025 (GHMU 2025) is a gathering of gamers from all corners of the world. It's a chance to meet and connect with like-minded individuals who share their gaming interests. Join us for a fun and exciting gaming experience!

Information

The event will take place at the iconic game hub, The Gaming Hub, located in the heart of the city.

The Gaming Hub is a state-of-the-art venue with the latest gaming hardware and software, perfect for a day of competitive gaming and networking with fellow gamers. The event will have a variety of games to play, including popular titles like League of Legends, Dota 2, and Overwatch, as well as some indie gems. There will also be a prize pool of $10,000 and a raffle with gaming gear and accessories.

Date: 2025-10-01
Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Price: Free
